Eggs Benedict

Description

Eggs Benedict is a common American brunch dish originating in New York City. Consisting of an english muffin, a cured pork product, poached eggs, and hollandaise sauce, Eggs Benedict is commonly considered a test of a chef's artistry, as it uses simple ingredients, but complex cooking methods.

The following recipe is a more upscale version, which I learned from our lord and savior Gordon Ramsay himself.

Ingredients

Hollandaise

  • 3 eggs
  • 1 stick butter

Eggs Benedict

  • 1 English muffin
  • 2 slices pancetta
  • 2 eggs
  • Hollandaise
  • Chives (for garnish)
