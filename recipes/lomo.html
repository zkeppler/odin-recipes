Lomo Saltado

Description

Lomo Saltado is a Peruvian dish consisting of tender steak and vegetables in a savory sauce served with rice and french fries. Peru has a long and rich tradition of chinese cooking, owing to the prevalence of Chinese immigrants in the country dating back to the late 19th century. Lomo wears its chinese influence on its sleeve, using the Chinese cooking technique of stir frying, which is implied in the name "saltado".

I learned this recipe from my father in law, who grew up in Peru and came to the United States in the late 1970s.

Ingredients

Lomo Saltado

  • 1.5 lbs tender steak, such as skirt steak, sirloin, or tenderloin
  • 1 red onion
  • 1 yellow bell pepper
  • 3 roma tomatoes
  • 1 tbsp garlic, minced
  • 1 tbsp aji amarillo paste (Adjust to your preferred spice level. I like more spice, my wife likes less)
  • 2 tbsp red wine vinegar
  • 2 tbsp soy sauce
  • Salt and pepper
  • Cilantro (for garnish)

Cilantro Rice

  • 1 cup dry long grain white rice
  • 1/4 cup cilantro, chopped
  • 1/4 cup lime juice, or to taste
  • Salt and pepper, to taste

Fench Fries

  • 2 russett potatoes
  • 1 liter peanut oil
  • Salt

Steps

Lomo Saltado

  1. Slice steak, onion, tomatoes, and bell pepper into thin, even strips.
  2. Season steak with salt, pepper, and minced garlic.
  3. Heat a wok over high heat until ripping hot, then sear steak strips, cooking in batches so as to not overcrowd the pan. Set steak aside.
  4. Turn heat down to medium and add vegetables and aji paste. Stir fry until cooked through, but still a little crunchy.
  5. Add steak back to wok and stir ingredients together.
  6. Pour vinegar and soy sauce down side of wok and allow to thicken.
  7. Garnish with cilantro and serve alongside cilantro rice and french fries.

Cilantro Rice

  1. Prepare a pot of rice according to your chosen method. I prefer to use a rice cooker because it reliably cooks perfect rice every time.
  2. Transfer rice to a wooden bowl and fluff with a wooden spoon to cool down.
  3. Combine rice, cilantro, lime juice, salt, and pepper and serve.
